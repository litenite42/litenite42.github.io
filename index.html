Welcome, I've built this page to ramble on... I mean tell you about myself. I grew up in or around the Columbus, GA area. Without realizing it, my uncle got me hooked on electronics when he gave me his old GameBoy and a copy of Pokemon Red. Fascinated for hours (ok, years...), I wondered how it could work. I remember my dad's laugh years later when I called our new PlayStation 2, "The NEW Nintendo?!". Suffice it to say, computing has been a large part of my life from a young age whether I fully understood it or not. Robotics classes in high schools piqued my interest in the programming side of things, but it wasn't until my years at Columbus State University that the spark was lit. My engineering-focused cohort at the time moaned over the tedium of coding, but I was mesmerized by the limitless possibilities that lay, quite literally, at the tip of my fingers.
